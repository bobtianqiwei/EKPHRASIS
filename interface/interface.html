An interactive system for graphic design education. Create compositions and get "less" and "more" effective examples to build intuition for design vocabulary.

Paper (CHI EA ’25)
bobwei.top · sshen.info · shmuh.co

Create a composition with blocks in varying sizes and shades of grey.
Arrange them to achieve Visual Balance, either spaced apart or overlapping.
You draw one, then the computer draws one, until you reach Visual Balance.
Click and drag to draw rectangles. Drag a block to move it, drag a corner to resize it, and click a swatch to recolor the selected block.
